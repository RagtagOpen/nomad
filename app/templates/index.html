Tips for a Successful Carpool

  • Have a real live phone conversation with your driver or passengers before the day you’re scheduled to carpool. Have a conversation about expectations - who will chip in for gas? Who’s in charge of tunes? Who’s in charge of snacks?
  • Check out your driver’s or passengers’ social media profile(s) ahead of time.
  • Make sure a friend or family member knows where you’re going and who you’re riding with; set times to check in with them periodically during your carpool and canvass day.
  • Charge your phone before you leave on your trip. Bring your charger and an extra battery, just in case!

Canvassing Tips

  • Make sure your phone is fully charged before heading out on your canvass.
  • Make sure you’ve got your volunteer coordinator’s phone number in your (fully charged) phone.
  • Listen carefully to all instructions from the campaign organizers.
  • Stay safe! If you feel uncomfortable at any door, or with any person you encounter while canvassing, leave. Remember to let your volunteer coordinator know.
  • Don’t forget to bring water and snacks.
  • Wear comfy, durable shoes. You’re going to need them!
